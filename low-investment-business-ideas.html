10 Low Investment Business Ideas You Can Start Today

Practical, real-world business ideas that require minimal startup capital and can grow with consistent effort.

1. Mobile Phone Repair Service

Smartphones are essential and frequently need repairs — cracked screens, battery replacement, and software troubleshooting. A short technical course and basic tools are enough to start providing these services from home or through a small kiosk.

2. Home-Based Bakery or Snack Business

If you enjoy baking, start by taking orders via WhatsApp and social media. Offer a limited menu to maintain quality and scale up as demand grows. Focus on packaging, hygiene, and consistent taste.

3. Digital Marketing Services for Local Businesses

Many local shops need simple digital marketing help: setting up social pages, creating promotional posts, or running basic ads. With a laptop and internet connection you can offer monthly packages.

4. Tailoring and Clothing Alteration

Tailoring services — hemming, stitching, and alterations — are always in demand. Learn basic tailoring skills and offer pickup/delivery for convenience.

5. Tutoring and Coaching

Tutoring requires minimal investment: knowledge, patience, and a quiet space. Offer online or in-person lessons, and consider group classes to increase hourly earnings.

6. Grocery Delivery Service

Partner with local grocers and offer delivery to nearby neighborhoods. This service is especially valuable for elderly or busy customers and can be monetized per-delivery or via subscription.

7. Custom Gifts and Crafts

Handmade or customized gifts (printed mugs, T‑shirts, craft decor) sell well online and at local events. Use social platforms and WhatsApp groups to showcase samples and take orders.

8. Photography and Content Creation

Start with a smartphone and build a portfolio of event photos, product images for local businesses, or short marketing videos. As you grow, invest in better equipment and editing skills.

9. Cleaning Services (Homes and Offices)

Residential and commercial cleaning services have steady demand. Begin alone or with a small team, and offer specialized services like carpet or sofa cleaning to increase margins.

10. Reselling Products Online

Buy small lots from wholesalers and resell on social platforms. Focus on a niche (accessories, beauty products, kitchenware) and build repeat customers through good service.

Conclusion

Low-investment businesses succeed when you focus on reliability, quality, and strong local marketing. Start with a single, manageable offering; reinvest profits; and scale services as demand grows.
